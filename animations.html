About the filmmaker

Nick Niles is a filmmaker, musician and award-winning graphic designer living and working in Zürich, Switzerland. He studied in the Design & Technology masters program at Parsons School of Design in New York City, and received an MFA in Film Directing from Columbia University. During the day, he’s the Head of Product Design at Voice Republic. He’s passionate about helping people tell their stories. Nick was a co-creator of Plan Your Recovery's opioid treatment testimonials and animated videos on addiction and addiction pharmacotherapy.
